# Geography

Geography is the study of the Earth — its physical features, climate, countries, and the people who inhabit it. A strong understanding of geography is essential for competitive exams and builds a foundation for understanding current affairs and world events.

## What You Will Find Here

- **Physical Geography** — The Universe, Solar System, Earth's structure, Atmosphere, Landforms, Water Bodies
- **World Geography** — Countries, Continents, Climate, Natural Resources, Wonders of the World
- **Indian Geography** — Physical features, Rivers, States, Climate, Agriculture
