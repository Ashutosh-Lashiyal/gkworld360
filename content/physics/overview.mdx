# Physics

Physics is the branch of science that studies matter, energy, and the fundamental forces of nature. From the motion of planets to the flow of electricity, physics explains how the universe works.

## What You Will Find Here

- **Mechanics** — Matter, Mass, Force, Energy, Motion, Work
- **Heat** — Temperature, Radiation, Laws of Thermodynamics
- **Light** — Reflection, Refraction, Mirrors, Lenses
- **Sound** — Waves, Speed of Sound, Musical Instruments
- **Electricity & Magnetism** — Current, Circuits, Magnetic Fields
- **Laws of Physics** — Newton, Faraday, Ohm, Archimedes and more
