# Biology

Biology is the science of life — the study of living organisms, how they function, grow, reproduce, and interact with their environment. Biology is a key subject for competitive exams and forms the basis of medicine and environmental science.

## What You Will Find Here

- **Cell Biology** — Structure of the Cell, Cell Division
- **Classification** — Scientific Nomenclature, Living Organisms
- **Plant Kingdom** — Photosynthesis, Transpiration, Plant Parts
- **Animal Kingdom** — Protozoa, Metazoa, Classification
- **Biological Processes** — Metabolism, Respiration, Fertilization
- **Evolution** — Origin of Life, Theory of Evolution
