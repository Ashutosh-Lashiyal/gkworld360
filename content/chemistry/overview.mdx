# Chemistry

Chemistry is the science of matter — what substances are made of, how they interact, and what happens when they combine or break apart. Chemistry is essential for competitive exams and helps explain the world around us.

## What You Will Find Here

- **Elements & Metals** — Types of Elements, Metals, Non-metals, Alloys, Minerals
- **Chemical Compounds** — Reactions, Air, Water, Important Gases
- **Industrial Chemistry** — Soaps, Glass, Cement, Coal
- **Organic Chemistry** — Carbon, Petroleum, Rubber, Radioactivity
- **Laws & Processes** — Important chemical tests, laws, and processes
