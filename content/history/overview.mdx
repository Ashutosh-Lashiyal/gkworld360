# History

History is the study of past events, particularly in human affairs. Understanding history helps us make sense of the present and prepare for the future. For competitive exam aspirants and general knowledge enthusiasts, history is one of the most important and rewarding subjects to master.

## What You Will Find Here

GKWorld360's History section covers a broad and well-organised curriculum designed for both deep learning and quick revision:

- **Ancient India** — Indus Valley Civilisation, Vedic Age, Maurya and Gupta Empires
- **Medieval India** — Delhi Sultanate, Mughal Empire, Vijayanagara Kingdom
- **Modern India** — British rule, the freedom struggle, partition and independence
- **World History** — Ancient civilisations, World Wars, the Cold War, and beyond

## How to Study History Effectively

History becomes easy when you understand the **connections between events**, not just the dates. Ask yourself:

- *Why* did this event happen?
- *Who* were the key people involved?
- *What* changed because of it?

> "Those who cannot remember the past are condemned to repeat it." — George Santayana
